# E04.2 Role and credential lifecycle

Epic: [[Iris Epics]], E04 Roles, Grants and Credentials
Spec: [[Iris Specification Inventory]] (source of truth; on conflict the spec wins)

## Goal

Deliver the meta-side plumbing for one engine-owned Postgres role per pipeline: the grants access ledger and roles mapping shape in meta, engine-managed credentials held only for pipeline login roles, and role teardown that executes only as part of declare destroy and engine uninstall, riding their confirmation gates. This gives the reconcile and injection tasks a trustworthy ledger and credential store to build on.

## Depends on

Epics E02, E03. Nothing beyond the epic's own dependencies.
