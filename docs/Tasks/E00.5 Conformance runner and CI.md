# E00.5 Conformance runner and CI

Epic: [[Iris Epics]], E00 Conformance Harness and Traceability Gate
Spec: [[Iris Specification Inventory]] (source of truth; on conflict the spec wins)

## Goal

Close the epic with the live tier: the conformance runner drives the actual shipped binary against a running daemon over the socket, asserting exit codes and --json output against a real Postgres the engine creates itself. Wire the CI pipeline (traceability gate, lint, unit+integration matrix, build, conformance with a Postgres service container) as the merge gate, and record the doctrine that acceptance-scenario steps are the conformance suite for their section's contracts.

[[E00.2 Traceability gate]] (CI runs the gate) and [[E00.3 Golden files and fixtures]] (the golden workspace it drives).
