# E11.3 Promotion and self demotion

Epic: [[Iris Epics]], E11 High Availability and Failover
Spec: [[Iris Specification Inventory]] (source of truth; on conflict the spec wins)

## Goal

Deliver the leadership transition semantics: a newly promoted daemon runs the same startup reconciliation as a restart, and a daemon that loses its meta session self-demotes (stops dispatching, kills in-flight runs, re-enters standby on a fresh session). It also covers the in-flight run fate across failover: the new leader dead-letters in-flight runs without killing their processes, and failover-killed runs poison their dependents until an explicit deadletter replay.

## Depends on

Epics E02, E05; builds on [[E11.1 Leader lock election]] for lock and session semantics.
