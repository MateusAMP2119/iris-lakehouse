# E12.2 Info inspect and show

Epic: [[Iris Epics]], E12 Stats, Info and Inspect
Spec: [[Iris Specification Inventory]] (source of truth; on conflict the spec wins)

## Goal

Deliver the remaining read-only readouts: `iris engine info` with its full field set including leader/standby role, `iris engine inspect` as a mutation-free DDL dump, and `iris pipeline show` for a pipeline's declaration, role, grants, and recent runs. Close the epic's invariants across every readout: uptime as the engine's sole, display-only wall-clock, and no last-heartbeat or last-seen fields anywhere (connection state is the only liveness signal).

## Depends on

Epics E02, E05; builds on [[E12.1 Stats rollups]] (the no-liveness invariant also covers the stats readout).
