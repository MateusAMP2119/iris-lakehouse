# E13.4 Journal capture and wipe

Epic: [[Iris Epics]], E13 Golden Sample and Acceptance

## Goal

Prove the journal drives the data story on the golden sample: wipe reverts exactly what the dev/disposable run landed, promoted writes stay captured but wipe-immune, concurrent writers journal in commit order with the last committed writer named as author, and the definition-of-done capture budget holds (a 10M-row promoted insert within 1.25x of the capture-less baseline).

## Depends on

All other epics as they land (E13 is the spine that proves them); builds on the dev run from [[E13.3 Lane runs and failures]].
