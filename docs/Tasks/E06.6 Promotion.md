# E06.6 Promotion

Epic: [[Iris Epics]], E06 Write Capture, Wipe and Promotion
Spec: [[Iris Specification Inventory]] (source of truth; on conflict the spec wins)

## Goal

Deliver promotion as a marker-only flip: open journal entries become undo = promoted, the pipeline's data leaves wipe eligibility, and no table row or journal entry is copied, moved, or deleted. Capture and provenance continue unchanged afterward at stamp cost, upholding the one-store doctrine that disposable and permanent rows live in the declared tables themselves.

## Depends on

Epics E03, E05. Builds on [[E06.5 Wipe replay and conflicts]] (shares the undo marker lifecycle).

## Contracts (5 testable, 1 exempt)

| Contract | Behavior | Tier |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `S01/promote-ends-wipe-eligibility` | Promotion removes the pipeline's data from wipe eligibility and changes nothing else about the pipeline. | unit |
| `S05/promotion-flips-open-to-promoted` | Promotion flips the pipeline's open journal entries to undo = promoted, and subsequent wipes skip them. | integration |
