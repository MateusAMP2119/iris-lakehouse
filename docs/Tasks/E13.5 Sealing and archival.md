# E13.5 Sealing and archival

Epic: [[Iris Epics]], E13 Golden Sample and Acceptance
Spec: [[Iris Specification Inventory]] (source of truth; on conflict the spec wins)

## Goal

Close the journal partition lifecycle on the golden sample: sealing waits for in-flight runs so the journal window stays whole, compaction nulls released pre-images and folds duplicate stamps, the sealed partition exports to the object store and drops from Postgres, and the checkpoint chain validates by digest and signature afterward.

## Depends on

All other epics as they land (E13 is the spine that proves them); builds on journaled writes from [[E13.4 Journal capture and wipe]].
