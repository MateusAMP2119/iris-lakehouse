# E11.1 Leader lock election

Epic: [[Iris Epics]], E11 High Availability and Failover
Spec: [[Iris Specification Inventory]] (source of truth; on conflict the spec wins)

## Goal

Deliver the core leadership mechanism: leadership is a Postgres session advisory lock, standby candidates block acquiring it, and no meta write is ever issued over a session that has not re-acquired the lock. The lock logic is driven against the meta-store fake per the epic's cutting notes, keeping this task unit-heavy and daemon-free. It also records the epic's three scope statements (managed Postgres dies with its host, no scale-out by design, Postgres is the scaling ceiling) as exempt manifest entries.

## Depends on

Epics E02, E05.

| Contract | Behavior | Tier |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `S15/advisory-lock-leader-election` | Leadership is a Postgres session advisory lock: a standby blocked acquiring the meta leader lock acquires it and becomes leader when the previous holder's session ends. | integration |
