# E14.3 Rail renderer and golden files

Epic: [[Iris Epics]], E14 Graph Views and Triage Surface
Spec: [[Iris Specification Inventory]] (source of truth; on conflict the spec wins)

## Goal

The git-graph lineage rendering for iris run list --graph and its honesty contract. The rail renderer draws a solid stroke only for run_inputs edges, dots same-pipeline serial order, keeps id gaps visible, annotates replays without edges, and refuses to weave past the rail cap. The --ascii renders are pinned byte-for-byte by golden files. The doctrine holds that wiring and lineage are two graphs that never share a rendering.

## Depends on

Epics E05, E07, E09. Builds before E13, the acceptance spine.
