# E09.1 PAT store and scopes

Epic: [[Iris Epics]], E09 Read API, Endpoints and PATs
Spec: [[Iris Specification Inventory]] (source of truth; on conflict the spec wins)

## Goal

Deliver the PAT foundation: the pats and pat_scopes store shapes, mint with argon2id hashing and show-once printing, scope subset validation, and effective authority as the union of scope rows. Includes the data-PAT side of minting: grant resolution at mint time and the engine-managed read-only NOLOGIN Postgres role recorded in the access ledger, with no credentials row. This is the auth substrate every route task builds on.

## Depends on

Epics E02, E03, E04.
