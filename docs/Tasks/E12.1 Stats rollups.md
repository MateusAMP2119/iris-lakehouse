# E12.1 Stats rollups

Epic: [[Iris Epics]], E12 Stats, Info and Inspect
Spec: [[Iris Specification Inventory]] (source of truth; on conflict the spec wins)

## Goal

Deliver the read-only stats rollup: the engine-wide, per-lane, and per-pipeline payloads, served identically over `iris engine stats` and GET /stats. Enforce the payload's purity rules (clock-free counts and last-values only, checkpoint chain head included) and the lifecycle of the leader-held per-lane pass counter, and confirm core exposes no /metrics endpoint.

## Depends on

Epics E02, E05.
