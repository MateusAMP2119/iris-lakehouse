# E06.5 Wipe replay and conflicts

Epic: [[Iris Epics]], E06 Write Capture, Wipe and Promotion
Spec: [[Iris Specification Inventory]] (source of truth; on conflict the spec wins)

## Goal

Implement the wipe algorithm as pure units over journal fixtures: scope selection of open disposable entries, reverse-order replay that deletes disposable inserts and restores pre-images, the journal-internal conflict-skip rule (any later entry for the same row wins, no image comparison), and retiring every visited entry to undo = wiped so conflicts report once. Includes rolling a run's cursor-advance write back together with its data.

## Depends on

Epics E03, E05. Builds on [[E06.1 Journal DDL and partitioning]] (journal fixtures follow its shape).
